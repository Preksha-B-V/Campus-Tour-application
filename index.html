About CMR University

CMR University is committed to academic excellence and innovation.The Motto of CMRU is Nurturing creative thinkers to drive positive global change. CMR University (CMRU) is a private university located in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. Established in 2013 under the CMR University Act, it offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, law, architecture, and social sciences. The campus covers an area of 60 acres and is recognized by UGC and accredited by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU)
